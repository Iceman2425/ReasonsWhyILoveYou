Babe, you are the sweetest storm I've ever known. One moment, you're all heart—thoughtful, gentle, checking in to make sure I've eaten or reminding me to ensure I take care of myself like I'm five. You've got this way of loving that feels like home—warm, safe, and a little addictive. But then—flip! In the blink of an eye, you can go from “sweetheart” to “storm warning.” One wrong word, and it's like the forecast suddenly called for thunder. And honestly? I kind of love it. That fire is part of your charm. You feel everything deeply—whether it's love, joy, or righteous frustration when I forget to switch over the laundry... again. You keep me on my toes, keep life interesting, and you make sure I never get too comfortable. And even in your most dramatic moments, I'd choose you a thousand times over because nothing compares to loving someone who feels it all and gives it all. You're my beautiful, unpredictable, incredible woman—and I wouldn't have it any other way.
